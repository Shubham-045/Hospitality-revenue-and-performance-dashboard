# Hospitality Revenue & Performance Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing hospitality revenue and operational performance using Power BI. It provides an interactive dashboard to monitor key hotel metrics and support data-driven decision-making in areas such as pricing, occupancy, and revenue optimization.

The objective is to convert raw hospitality data into clear and actionable business insights through effective visualization.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Evaluate overall revenue performance  
- Track key hospitality KPIs such as RevPAR, ADR, Occupancy %, and DSRN  
- Identify top-performing cities and hotel properties  
- Compare revenue across Luxury and Business segments  
- Analyze booking platforms based on ADR and Realisation %  
- Study weekly trends for better strategic planning  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- **Revenue** – Total revenue generated  
- **RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room)** – Measures revenue efficiency  
- **ADR (Average Daily Rate)** – Average revenue per occupied room  
- **Occupancy %** – Percentage of rooms occupied  
- **DSRN (Daily Sellable Room Nights)**  
- **DBRN (Daily Booked Room Nights)**  
- **DURN (Daily Utilized Room Nights)**  
- **Realisation %** – Revenue retained after cancellations/discounts
